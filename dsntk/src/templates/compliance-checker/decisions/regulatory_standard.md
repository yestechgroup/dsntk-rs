# Regulatory Standard

Reference to the applicable regulatory framework governing product compliance.

## Required certifications

All products must hold **ISO 9001** and **CE** certifications for European markets.

## Restricted countries

Products may not be sold to countries on the sanctions list. Country-specific
requirements are maintained by the Compliance Department and updated quarterly.
